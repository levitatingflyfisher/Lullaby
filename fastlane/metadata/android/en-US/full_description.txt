Lullaby is a free, open-source baby tracker that keeps all your data on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no ads, no tracking.

Track everything in one place:
• Feeding — breast, bottle, and solids with live timer and left/right toggle
• Sleep — nap and night sessions with start/stop timer
• Diapers — wet, dirty, mixed, with stool color
• Growth — weight, height, and head circumference plotted on WHO percentile curves
• Health — medicine doses and vaccination records
• Statistics — feeding trends, sleep patterns, and diaper frequency charts
• Calendar — browse events by day
• Timeline — scrollable activity history with filter chips
• Doctor summary — at-a-glance report you can share as PDF or CSV at appointments

More features:
• Encrypted backup — protect your data with a 12-word recovery phrase
• Home screen widget — see last feed, sleep, and diaper at a glance with live timer
• Multiple babies — manage profiles for more than one child
• Dark mode — follows your system theme or set manually
• Material You — dynamic color theming on supported devices

Built by a nonprofit. No ads ever. No data collection ever.
